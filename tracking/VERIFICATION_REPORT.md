A decisive independent cross-check (Copernicus GFM / Sentinel-2 optical on 06-02/03/07) is recommended at the post-harvest re-run. The summer harvest/fire peak is unobserved, so every headline figure is a LOWER BOUND. The pipeline is reproducible by design: re-run post-harvest (≈ Jul–Aug 2026, better ≈ Oct) for the concluded full-year result. Field/expert verification is the gold standard above remote-sensing self-consistency.
